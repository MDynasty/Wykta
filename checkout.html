Where does money go after payment?

Funds are routed through the selected payment gateway and automatically transferred to your configured payout account.

  • Card/Apple Pay/Google Pay → Stripe balance → connected bank account
  • PayPal → PayPal Business balance → linked bank payout
  • Alipay/WeChat Pay → configured PSP merchant settlement account
  • USDT (TRC20) → merchant wallet address: TRC20_WYKTA_MERCHANT_WALLET
  • Wire transfer → business IBAN/SWIFT account configured by finance
